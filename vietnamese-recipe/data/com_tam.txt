RECIPE: Com Tam (Broken Rice with Grilled Pork)
IMAGE: com-tam.jpg
REGION: Southern
TIME: 60
SERVINGS: 4
CALORIES: 600
DIFFICULTY: Medium
CATEGORY: Rice
TAGS: saigon style, breakfast, grilled pork, savory
INGREDIENTS:
- 2 cups broken rice
- 500g pork chops
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 shallot, minced
- Black pepper
- Fried eggs
- Pickled vegetables
- Cucumber and tomato slices
- Scallion oil
- Fish sauce dipping
STEPS:
- Marinate pork with fish sauce, sugar, garlic, shallot, and pepper for 1 hour
- Cook broken rice with slightly more water than regular rice
- Grill pork chops until charred and cooked through
- Fry eggs sunny side up
- Serve rice with grilled pork, fried egg, pickled vegetables, cucumber, tomato
- Drizzle with scallion oil and serve with fish sauce
