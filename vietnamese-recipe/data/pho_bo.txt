RECIPE: Pho Bo (Beef Noodle Soup)
REGION: Northern
TIME: 180
SERVINGS: 4
CALORIES: 450
DIFFICULTY: Medium
CATEGORY: Soup
TAGS: noodle, beef, traditional, breakfast
INGREDIENTS:
- 2 lbs beef bones
- 1 lb beef brisket
- 1 onion, charred
- 3 inch ginger, charred
- 2 cinnamon sticks
- 3 star anise
- 4 cloves
- 1 tbsp coriander seeds
- 400g rice noodles
- Fresh herbs (basil, cilantro, mint)
- Bean sprouts
- Lime wedges
- Hoisin sauce, sriracha
STEPS:
- Blanch bones and beef in boiling water for 5 minutes, then rinse
- Char onion and ginger over flame until fragrant
- Toast spices in dry pan until aromatic
- Simmer bones, beef, charred aromatics, and spices in 4L water for 3 hours
- Remove beef after 1 hour, slice thinly
- Strain broth, season with fish sauce and sugar
- Cook rice noodles according to package
- Assemble noodles, beef slices, hot broth
- Serve with fresh herbs, bean sprouts, lime, and sauces
