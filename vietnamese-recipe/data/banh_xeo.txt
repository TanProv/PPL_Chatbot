RECIPE: Banh Xeo (Crispy Vietnamese Crepes)
IMAGE: banh-xeo.jpg
REGION: Southern
TIME: 40
SERVINGS: 4
CALORIES: 450
DIFFICULTY: Medium
CATEGORY: Main
TAGS: pancake, crispy, coconut milk, wrap
INGREDIENTS:
- 1 cup rice flour
- 1/2 cup coconut milk
- 1 cup water
- 1/4 tsp turmeric
- 200g pork belly, sliced
- 200g shrimp, peeled
- Bean sprouts
- Onion, sliced
- Scallions
- Lettuce, herbs
- Fish sauce dip
STEPS:
- Mix rice flour, coconut milk, water, and turmeric into thin batter
- Rest batter 30 minutes
- Heat oil in non-stick pan
- Saute pork and shrimp briefly
- Pour thin layer of batter, swirl to coat
- Add pork, shrimp, bean sprouts, onion on half
- Cover and cook until crispy and golden
- Fold in half
- Serve with lettuce, herbs, and fish sauce
