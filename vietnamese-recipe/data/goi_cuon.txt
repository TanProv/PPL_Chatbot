RECIPE: Goi Cuon (Fresh Spring Rolls)
REGION: Southern
TIME: 30
SERVINGS: 6
CALORIES: 150
DIFFICULTY: Easy
CATEGORY: Appetizer
TAGS: healthy, fresh, low carb, shrimp
INGREDIENTS:
- 12 rice paper wrappers
- 200g cooked shrimp
- 200g cooked pork belly, sliced
- Rice vermicelli, cooked
- Lettuce leaves
- Fresh herbs (mint, cilantro, Thai basil)
- Cucumber, julienned
- Peanut dipping sauce
STEPS:
- Soften rice paper in warm water for 10 seconds
- Place on damp towel
- Layer lettuce, herbs, vermicelli, shrimp, pork, and cucumber on lower third
- Fold sides in and roll tightly
- Cut in half diagonally
- Serve with peanut sauce
