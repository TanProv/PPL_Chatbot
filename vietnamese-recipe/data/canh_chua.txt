RECIPE: Canh Chua (Sour Soup)
REGION: Southern
TIME: 30
SERVINGS: 4
CALORIES: 150
DIFFICULTY: Easy
CATEGORY: Soup
TAGS: sour, fish soup, tamarind, healthy
INGREDIENTS:
- 400g fish, cut into pieces
- 2 tomatoes, quartered
- 100g pineapple, cubed
- 100g okra, sliced
- 50g bean sprouts
- Tamarind paste
- Fish sauce
- Sugar
- Fresh herbs
- Chili
STEPS:
- Make tamarind water with paste
- Bring to boil with tomatoes
- Season with fish sauce and sugar
- Add pineapple and okra
- Add fish pieces, cook until done
- Add bean sprouts
- Garnish with herbs and chili
- Serve with steamed rice
