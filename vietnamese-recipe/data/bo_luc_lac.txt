RECIPE: Bo Luc Lac (Shaking Beef)
IMAGE: bo-luc-lac.jpg
REGION: Southern
TIME: 30
SERVINGS: 4
CALORIES: 400
DIFFICULTY: Easy
CATEGORY: Main
TAGS: beef, wok, party food, french inspired
INGREDIENTS:
- 600g beef sirloin, cubed
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Black pepper
- 1 onion, cut into chunks
- Cherry tomatoes
- Watercress or lettuce
- Lime pepper salt dip
STEPS:
- Marinate beef with garlic, soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, pepper for 15 minutes
- Heat wok until smoking hot
- Sear beef cubes in batches, tossing frequently
- Add onion and tomatoes, toss briefly
- Remove when beef is medium rare
- Serve on bed of watercress or lettuce
- Provide lime pepper salt dip on the side
