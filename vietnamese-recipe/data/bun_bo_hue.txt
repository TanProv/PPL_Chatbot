RECIPE: Bun Bo Hue (Spicy Beef Noodle Soup)
IMAGE: bun-bo-hue.jpg
REGION: Central
TIME: 240
SERVINGS: 6
CALORIES: 500
DIFFICULTY: Hard
CATEGORY: Soup
TAGS: spicy, hue style, beef, lemongrass
INGREDIENTS:
- 2 lbs beef shank
- 1 lb pork hock
- 500g beef bones
- 2 stalks lemongrass, crushed
- 2 tbsp annatto seeds
- 3 tbsp shrimp paste
- 2 tbsp chili oil
- Rock sugar
- Fish sauce
- 600g bun bo hue noodles
- Fresh herbs, lime, bean sprouts
- Banana blossom, sliced
- Vietnamese ham
STEPS:
- Blanch beef shank, pork hock, and bones
- Infuse oil with annatto seeds for red color
- Saute lemongrass and shrimp paste in annatto oil
- Add 4L water, meats, and bones, simmer 3 hours
- Season with fish sauce, sugar, and chili oil
- Cook noodles separately
- Slice meats thinly
- Assemble with noodles, meats, broth
- Serve with herbs, lime, bean sprouts, banana blossom
