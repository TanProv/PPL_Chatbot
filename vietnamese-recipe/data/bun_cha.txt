RECIPE: Bun Cha (Grilled Pork with Noodles)
REGION: Northern
TIME: 45
SERVINGS: 4
CALORIES: 550
DIFFICULTY: Easy
CATEGORY: Noodle
TAGS: hanoi style, grilled pork, dipping sauce, lunch
INGREDIENTS:
- 500g ground pork
- 300g pork belly, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 shallots, minced
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Black pepper
- 400g rice vermicelli
- Fresh herbs (mint, perilla, lettuce)
- Pickled papaya or carrot
- Dipping sauce (fish sauce, sugar, lime, garlic, chili)
STEPS:
- Mix ground pork with half the garlic, shallots, fish sauce, sugar, and pepper
- Form into small patties
- Marinate pork belly slices with remaining seasonings
- Grill both patties and belly slices until charred and cooked
- Cook vermicelli according to package
- Prepare dipping sauce by mixing all sauce ingredients
- Serve noodles with grilled pork, herbs, pickles, and dipping sauce
