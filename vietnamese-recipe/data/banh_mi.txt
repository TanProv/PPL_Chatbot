RECIPE: Banh Mi (Vietnamese Sandwich)
REGION: Southern
TIME: 30
SERVINGS: 4
CALORIES: 400
DIFFICULTY: Easy
CATEGORY: Sandwich
TAGS: street food, pork, bread, quick
INGREDIENTS:
- 4 Vietnamese baguettes
- 300g pork shoulder, sliced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Pickled daikon and carrots
- Cucumber slices
- Fresh cilantro
- Jalapeno slices
- Mayonnaise
- Pate (optional)
STEPS:
- Marinate pork with soy sauce, fish sauce, and sugar for 15 minutes
- Grill or panfry pork until cooked through
- Toast baguettes until crispy
- Spread mayonnaise and pate inside bread
- Layer with pork, pickled vegetables, cucumber, cilantro, and jalapeno
- Serve immediately while bread is crispy
