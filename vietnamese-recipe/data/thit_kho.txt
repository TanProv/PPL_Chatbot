RECIPE: Thit Kho (Caramelized Pork and Eggs)
REGION: Southern
TIME: 60
SERVINGS: 4
CALORIES: 500
DIFFICULTY: Easy
CATEGORY: Main
TAGS: tet holiday, pork belly, eggs, comfort food
INGREDIENTS:
- 600g pork belly, cubed
- 4 hard-boiled eggs, peeled
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 shallots, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Black pepper
- Coconut water
- Scallions
STEPS:
- Caramelize sugar in pot until dark
- Add fish sauce carefully
- Add shallots and garlic, saute briefly
- Add pork, coat with caramel
- Add coconut water to cover
- Add eggs and pepper
- Simmer covered 45 minutes until tender
- Reduce sauce until thick
- Garnish with scallions
- Serve with steamed rice
