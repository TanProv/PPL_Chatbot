RECIPE: Cha Gio (Fried Spring Rolls)
REGION: Southern
TIME: 45
SERVINGS: 6
CALORIES: 250
DIFFICULTY: Medium
CATEGORY: Appetizer
TAGS: crispy, fried, party food, pork
INGREDIENTS:
- 20 rice paper wrappers
- 300g ground pork
- 100g shrimp, minced
- 50g wood ear mushrooms, chopped
- 50g glass noodles, soaked and chopped
- 1 carrot, shredded
- 1 onion, minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Fish sauce, sugar, pepper
- Oil for frying
STEPS:
- Mix all filling ingredients with seasonings
- Soften rice paper briefly in water
- Place 2 tbsp filling on wrapper, fold sides and roll tightly
- Heat oil to 350F
- Fry rolls in batches until golden brown, about 5 minutes
- Drain on paper towels
- Serve with lettuce, herbs, and fish sauce dip
