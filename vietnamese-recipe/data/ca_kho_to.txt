RECIPE: Ca Kho To (Caramelized Fish in Clay Pot)
REGION: Southern
TIME: 45
SERVINGS: 4
CALORIES: 350
DIFFICULTY: Medium
CATEGORY: Main
TAGS: fish, caramelized, salty, family dinner
INGREDIENTS:
- 600g catfish, cut into chunks
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 shallots, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 chili, sliced
- Black pepper
- Coconut water
- Scallions, chopped
STEPS:
- Make caramel by heating sugar until dark brown
- Add fish sauce carefully (will bubble)
- Add shallots, garlic, and chili to clay pot
- Arrange fish pieces on top
- Pour caramel sauce over fish
- Add coconut water to barely cover
- Simmer covered for 30 minutes
- Season with pepper
- Garnish with scallions
- Serve with steamed rice
